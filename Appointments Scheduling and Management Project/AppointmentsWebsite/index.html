Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.

How can I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by visiting our website and selecting a convenient date and time slot.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring your insurance card, identification, and any relevant medical records or test results.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment by logging into your account on our website or contacting our clinic directly.

Is there a fee for cancelling an appointment?

There may be a fee for late cancellations or no-show appointments. Please refer to our cancellation policy for more information.

How early should I arrive for my appointment?

We recommend arriving at least 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time to complete any necessary paperwork and check-in procedures.
